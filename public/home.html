We are a University of Florida student organization in association with the Seventh-day Adventist church, providing a Christian atmosphere for those who choose to take part. Our missions is to share the love of Jesus Christ and spread the Advent message, primarily to the University of Florida students and secondarily to the community of Gainesville. To represent the love of Jesus Christ by having no prejudice to sex, race, or religious background, of not only those we help and support on our campus, but also to those who wish to attend our meetings and special events. We are planning to accomplish these goals by organizing open campus activities and Bible discussions, fellowship, and large events.
